Weather

This project explored the relationship between weather conditions and the latitude of a collection of 600+ randomly selected cities. After pulling weather data from the OpenWeatherMap API, I utilized Matplotlib to plot several comparisons between weather conditions and the latitude of the cities, including temperature, cloudiness, wind speed, and humidity. In addition to the visualizations, you will find the dataset as well as trends and correlations that can be observed.
